We just wanted to take a moment to thank organizing committee of the conference, both technical and finical sponsors, keynote and special session speakers, reviewers, authors, top management of Bahir Dar Institute of Technology and others involved in the conference for such a great support! A Training on AI and Its Applications was provided to Shumaba High School and Preparatory School

ICT4D Research Center has provided a two-hour training on Artificial Intelligence(AI) and it's applications to Shumaba High School and Preparatory School students on December 7, 2014. In his opening remarks, Coordinator of Training, Mr. Yesuneh Getachew, highlighted the importance Artificial Intelligence and its potential for the future. Mr. Binyam Hailu provided detailed information on the nature on AI Technology, current state of AI and its applications, etc. He also pointed out what can be done next by citing the problem-solving works done using AI.

The 3rd International conference on ICT for Development for Africa was successfully conducted

The 3rdInternational Conference on Information and Communication Technology for Development for Africa (ICT4DA2021), was held in Bahir Dar, Ethiopia from 22 to 24 November, 2021. Keynote Speakers, panelists, authors, as well as delegates from sponsors, ICT-related governmental and non-governmental offices, companies and organizations have participated in the conference.
