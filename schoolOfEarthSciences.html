School of Earth sciences (SES) commitment in groundwater well site investigation, drilling, development and supervision for BDU

An urgent need of water by BDU initiates SES to be engaged in groundwater exploration, water well drilling, development and supervision tasks. The director of SES plays a central role in motivating groundwater related professionals principally hydrogeologists and related subject (geophysics, RS and GIS and Structural geology) experts to start the exploration.
