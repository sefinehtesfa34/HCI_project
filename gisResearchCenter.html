Remote Sensing training of trainers commences

Geospatial Data & Technology Center (GDTC) of Bahir Dar University in partnership with Ethiopian Educational Network to Support Agricultural Transformation (EENSAT) project offers a Remote sensing training for Bahir Dar Poly Technic College staff and Development Agents from six woreds (Yilmana Densa, Sekela, Mecha, Ebinat, Libokemkem, and Fogera). It is learnt that the training will last for four days (May18-21, 2021).
Topics the training is covering include:
• Best experiences from the previous trainings
• Fundamentals of Remote Sensing

Geospatial Data & Technology Center (GDTC) of Bahir Dar University in collaboration with Ethiopian Educational Network to Support Agricultural Transformation (EENSAT) project offers five days long hands-on GIS training for Natural Resource Managers working at Woreda (District) level.
