Supervision and followups

BENEFIT-REALISE EXPERTS supervise target woredas 2020 activities for technical support.

Most Target woredas and kebeles applied cluster approach. For instance, in Tach Gayint Qotmender kebele, one timad package wheat cluster covered 3.5 ha for 14 beneficiaries in one place and a bit away another cluster covered 4 ha for 16 beneficiaries.

Similarly, in Enzit kebele, wheat pre scaling up cluster called Wanza wheat cluster covered 11.25 ha for 45 beneficiaries while Qega wheat cluster covered area of 7.5 ha for 30 farmers.
