Call for application for ToT in Business Entrepreneurship

kumeadmin - Tue, 03/16/2021 - 01:15

Partnership in Business Entrepreneurship and Leadership Transformation (PiBELT) is one of the capacity building projects in Bahir Dar University. It has been executed in partnership with Indiana University, Ivy Tech Community College and Debre Markos Universitysince September 2020. One of the objectives of the project is to build the capacity of young science and technology innovators and entrepreneurs in the university and surrounding community through pre-acceleration and acceleration trainings.
