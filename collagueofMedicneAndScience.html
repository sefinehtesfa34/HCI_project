Professor Kassa Darge (MD, PhD), Pediatrics Radiologist, Delivers Lecture on Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) to Clinical Radiology and Imaging Residents and staff.

published by cohsadmin on Tue, 10/19/2021 - 11:04

(October 19, 2021, Bahir Dar): Adjunct Professor of College of Medicine and Health Sciences, Bahir Dar University, Professor Kassa Darge (MD, PhD), Radiologist-in Chief, The William L. Van Alen Endowed Radiology Chair, Professor of Radiology and Surgery, Department of Radiology, University of Pennsylvania, USA delivered lecture on Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) to Clinical Radiology and Imaging Residents and the staff today at Tibebe Ghion Campus.

He was warmly welcomed and received by Professor Yeshigeta Gelaw, Chief Executive Director of College of Medicine and Health Sciences and staff members.

The lecture started with an introduction speech provided by Dr. Anduamlak Bazezew, Head Department of Radiology and Imaging. In his speech, Dr. Andualmalk welcomed Prof. Kassa and introduced him to the staff and residents. He also stated the purpose and importance of the lecture and the experience the professor would share with them. He expressed that the lecture would improve the knowledge and the skills of the attendees and strengthened the service provision of Radiology Department at Tibebe Ghion Specialized Hospital.

In addition to the lecture, Professor Kassa shared his rich experiences with the staff and residents. He offered adequate explanations on a wide range of topics that are supposed to have huge impacts on the quality of clinical service provisions, academic and research works.

Following the lecture, Professor Kassa paid a visit to Tibebe Ghion Specialized Hospital. Particularly, he visited the Radiology and Imaging Department. In his visit, Professor Kassa, expressed that he was completely overwhelmed by what he observed. He reiterated that he found the college and the hospital more attractive than he had expected. He expressed his delight on the great set up of the radiology department.

College of Medicine and Health Sciences signs Memorandom of Understanding (MoU) with Universidad De Leon

(January 20, 2022, Bahir Dar): Bahir Dar University College of Medicine and Health Sciences has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Universidad De Leon to cooperate for exchange of students and/or staff in the context of the Erasmus+ programme.

In the partnership agreement that allows the exchange of students and staff between the two institutions, they have agreed to respect the quality requirements of the Erasmus Charter for Higher Education in all aspects of the organization and management of the mobility.
